Unfollow Checker

Find out who isn't following you back on Instagram. Everything runs locally in your browser — no data is ever uploaded.

How to get your Instagram JSON files:
  1. Open Instagram → Settings → Your activity → Download your information
  2. Select JSON format and request the download
  3. Extract the zip — look for followers_1.json and following.json
  4. Upload both files below and click Analyze
